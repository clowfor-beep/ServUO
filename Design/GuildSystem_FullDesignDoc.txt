Created: 2026-05-23 | Updated: 2026-05-24

OVERVIEW
--------
12 guilds across 3 tiers populate the world with AI-controlled
SimPlayers that behave like real player organisations.
Each guild has 12 members. 144 SimPlayers total.
Approximately 30 active at any given time.
Average 3-4 members online per guild.

POWER TIER SUMMARY
------------------
Tier 1 guilds     : Low skills, safe zones, non-threatening
Tier 2 guilds     : 7x100 = 700 skill cap, StatCap 180
Tier 3 standard   : 7x100 = 700 skill cap, StatCap 225
Blood Pact        : 8x100 = 800 skill cap, StatCap 250
Shadowblade       : 8x100 = 800 skill cap, StatCap 250
The Void          : 5x120+2x100+1x50 = 850, StatCap 270
Dread Hunters     : 5x120+2x100+1x80 = 880, StatCap 300 (max 125/stat)

GUILD 10: BLOOD PACT
----------------------------------------------------------------
Alignment    : Red (full murderers)
Real players : Hostile in Tier 2/3 — CALCULATED.
               Never attacks if odds unfavourable.
               Instant death in cities — never enter.
Rivals       : Iron Company, Silver Wolves, Paladin Order
               Dead Watchers (feared — avoid unless overwhelming)
Gate following: None
City gates   : Instant guard death — never
Random daily online roster: 2-7 members
Respawn timer: 20 minutes (stat loss consequence)
Skill cap    : 8x100 = 800, StatCap 250

ENGAGEMENT EVALUATION ALGORITHM:
  ENGAGE if:
    Blood Pact available >= target group size
    AND target health < 70% average
    AND escape route available
    AND no Dead Watchers or Paladin Order nearby
    AND loot value > stat loss risk
  WAIT if:
    Target health > 70%
    OR numbers are even
    OR Silver Wolves/Paladin Order in area
  RETREAT immediately if:
    Any member HP < 25%
    OR Dead Watchers arrive

ACTIVITIES (priority order):
1. Lurking near champion spawns — wait for Iron Company to weaken
2. Hunting solo real players in Tier 2/3
3. Evaluating group targets before engaging
4. Recalling immediately when losing
5. Calling reinforcements when viable
6. Thorough fast looting
7. Avoiding Dead Watchers
8. Wilderness resupply only

ROSTER AND RESPAWN SYSTEM
================================================================

NOTE: SimPlayers do NOT respawn. They RE-ACTIVATE after cooldown.
Pool PKs (FBPKSpawner) DO respawn. These are different systems.

Base online rate:    25-35% per guild (3-4 members average)
Prime time bonus:    +2 members (18:00-23:00 server time)
Off-peak reduction:  -1 member (00:00-08:00)

SimPlayer re-activation cooldowns after death:
  Tier 1 guilds      :  5 minutes
  Tier 2 guilds      : 10 minutes
  Tier 3 good        : 15 minutes
  Dread Hunters      : 15 minutes
  Blood Pact         : 20 minutes (stat loss consequence)
  The Void           : 20 minutes
  Shadowblade        : 15 minutes

ROSTER EXHAUSTION:
  Kill enough SimPlayers fast enough — guild runs dry.
  No more members active until cooldowns expire.
  Core gameplay mechanic. Players can tactically exhaust guilds.
