What We've Built

Custom Systems

Every mechanic has been tuned to reward skill, punish laziness, and make every encounter feel earned.

Skill Synergies

Related skills amplify each other. Anatomy + Healing pushes bandage heals far beyond vanilla. Forensics and Tracking reduce barding difficulty. Master multiple disciplines and feel the difference.

Cooldown HUD

A live heads-up display tracks every active skill cooldown in real time — provocation, peacemaking, discordance, healing, hiding, and more. No more guessing when you can act.

Graveyard Encounters

Enter the Britain graveyard in Felucca and a Player Killer portals in to hunt you. Seven archetypes. Each one relentless. The dead don't rest here, and neither will you.

Tiered PK System

PKs scale with your danger. Three tiers — Newbie, Advanced, Expert — with stats and gear to match. Defeat one and prove your worth. Survive long enough and the Expert hunters find you.

Classic Felucca Rules

Full loot. Open world PvP. No Trammel safety net. The world works the way it was meant to — with consequences. Respect your surroundings or pay the price.

Ongoing Development

New content ships regularly. Quests, dungeons, custom encounters, and world events are always in progress. This shard grows with its community.

Know Your Enemy

The Hunters

Seven archetypes. One purpose. When you enter dangerous ground, they come for you.

How to Play

Connect to the Shard

Download ClassicUO

Get the free ClassicUO client from github.com/ClassicUO. It runs on Windows, Mac, and Linux.

Get the UO Client Files

Download the free Ultima Online Classic Client from uo.com/client-download and install it. ClassicUO needs these data files to run.

Add the Server

In ClassicUO, click Add Server and enter the address and port below. Give your new account any username and password — it's created automatically.

Enter the World

Create your character and step into Britannia. The world is open. Watch your back in Felucca.
